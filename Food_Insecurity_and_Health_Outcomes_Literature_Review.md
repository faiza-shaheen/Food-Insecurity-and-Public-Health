# Food Insecurity and Public Health

This repository contains a literature-based research project examining the impact of food insecurity on nutritional status and health outcomes.

Type: Literature-based research project  
Field: Public Health / Nutrition  
Year: 2025  
Author: Faiza Shaheen  

## Project Overview
Food insecurity is a major social determinant of health affecting dietary quality, nutritional status, and overall health outcomes. This project synthesizes existing peer-reviewed literature to explore the relationship between food insecurity and nutrition-related health consequences.
